# Hero Images Setup

## Required Images

Place the following high-quality images in the `public/` directory:

1. **hero-1.jpg** - Main bridal makeup look (1920x1080px or larger)
   - Best bridal transformation
   - High quality, professional photography
   - Portrait or landscape orientation

2. **hero-2.jpg** - Event makeup (1920x1080px or larger)
   - Haldi, engagement, or reception look
   - Vibrant, colorful image

3. **hero-3.jpg** - Photoshoot or class image (1920x1080px or larger)
   - Professional photoshoot styling
   - Or makeup class/workshop image

## Image Requirements

- **Format**: JPG or WebP
- **Size**: Minimum 1920x1080px (Full HD)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or similar
- **Quality**: High resolution, professional photography
- **Optimization**: Compress before adding (use TinyPNG or similar)

## Where to Get Images

1. Download from your Instagram account (@mahas_makeover)
2. Use portfolio photos from your work
3. Professional photoshoot images

## Quick Setup

Once you have the images:
1. Rename them to `hero-1.jpg`, `hero-2.jpg`, `hero-3.jpg`
2. Place them in the `public/` directory
3. The website will automatically use them

## Temporary Placeholder

Until you add your images, the hero section will show a gradient background. Once you add the images, they will automatically appear with the new premium design.
